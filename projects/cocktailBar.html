Cocktail Bar

For this one-page cocktail bar site, I explored how far a visual identity can go by using only shape, colour and text - no logos, images or illustrations. Inspired by classical aesthetics such as Greek and Roman design, I created a dramatic, theatrical style that gives the bar a unique and memorable personality. The end result included a complete design concept.
