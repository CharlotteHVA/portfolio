BoekBuddies

BookBuddies turns hidden readers within the CMD community into a visible and connected group. Using QR codes and a gaming app, students can log books, connect over shared interests and participate in reading sprints. The platform is playful, social and inclusive - designed to activate a community that already exists but has yet to find its voice.
