Boeken Zoeker

Commissioned by the city of Amsterdam, this kiosk app helps secondary school students quickly find reading materials in their school library. The interface, designed for a fixed iPad station at the library entrance, is intuitive and student-friendly, and encourages literacy by matching readers to the right books. The project focuses on accessibility, personalisation and user flows.
