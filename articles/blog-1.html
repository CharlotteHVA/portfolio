Pixels, Code & Coffee. Let's Begin!

Hey there and welcome!

Thanks for getting involved. This space is all about the things I love most: making things with pixels, bringing ideas to life through code, and, of course, sipping coffee while doing it all. I'm a web developer and digital creator. I'm passionate about thoughtful design, clean development, and experimenting with what the web can be.

So, let me tell you why I built this portfolio.

This site is for all your project needs. It's my digital playground — where I test ideas, refine visuals and combine logic with creativity. I'm always curious about what happens when design meets function, whether it's designing a website using only shapes and colour, building a custom reading app, or exploring the 3D world through Blender.

What You'll Find Here

This blog is where I'll sometimes share:

→ Some details about the design/development process.
→ Some behind-the-scenes sketches, wireframes and prototypes.
→ Learning about tools, techniques and failures that turn into something cool.
→ Projects I'm currently working on for school, clients or just for fun.

Some posts might be polished. Others might be messy. That's the fun part – it's all part of growing as a maker.

Let's get creative and make something awesome!

If you're into web design, digital experiments, or just like seeing how things are made, I hope this space gives you something to take with you. Whether you're a creative, someone we're working with or you've just dropped by for a coffee, thanks for joining.

Let's build something meaningful. More to come soon.

– Charlo Moo
